AB  - Abstract The Seventh Tripartite Meeting of the Surgical Research Society held in conjunction with the Society of University Surgeons and the European Surgical Research Society took place on 6, 7 and 8 July 1988 at the University of Bristol. The meeting incorporated the 68th Meeting of the Surgical Research Society. A symposium entitled ?Screening for aortic aneurysm? preceded the free paper sessions. One hundred and ninety papers were read and the Patey Prize was awarded to Mr D. J. A. Scott for his presentation, entitled ?Application of multiple linear regression in the estimation of peripheral resistance?.
